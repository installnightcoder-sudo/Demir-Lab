**Contributing to Demir-Lab**

Thank you for contributing!

What can be added?

- Guitar tones
- Bass tones
- Amp settings
- Pedal chains
- Music gear information

Rules

- Be respectful.
- Use clear formatting.
- Include the gear used.
- Do not submit spam or AI-generated nonsense.
- Check that information is accurate.
